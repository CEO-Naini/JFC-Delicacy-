Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and ensure that your personal information is protected and used only for order processing.

How We Use Your Information: Your information helps us process orders, provide customer support, send promotional updates, and improve our website. Security Measures: We implement strict security protocols to protect your personal data from unauthorized access and cyber threats. All transactions are encrypted, and we adhere to industry best practices to safeguard your privacy.

Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized recommendations. You can manage cookie settings through your browser at any time.

Policy Updates: This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in legal requirements or business practices. We encourage you to review it regularly.
