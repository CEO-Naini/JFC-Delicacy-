About JFC Delicacy

Welcome to JFC Delicacy, your go-to destination for the finest and freshest food products. JFC Delicacy is a leading online food store delivering fresh and tasty meals to your doorstep. It was first launched in 2023 by James Kuyioni at Mwihoko, next to Nafuumart supermarket and we hope to build many more branches in the future. Our journey began with a passion for delivering high-quality, delicious meals and ingredients straight to your home. At JFC Delicacy, we believe that good food is the foundation of happiness, and we are committed to providing our customers with a wide variety of premium, hand-selected delicacies that cater to all tastes and preferences. We are open from monday to friday as at 7am-10pm and as from 10am to 6pm over the weekend.

Over the few years, we have grown from a small startup to a trusted name in the food industry, serving thousands of satisfied customers. Whether you're looking for gourmet ingredients, freshly baked goods, organic produce, or ready-to-eat meals, we have something for everyone. We prioritize quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, ensuring that every order meets the highest standards.

Our mission is simple: to bring people together through great food. We work closely with local farmers, artisans, and top suppliers to source the best products while supporting ethical and sustainable practices. With our easy-to-use online store, secure payment options, and reliable delivery service, shopping for your favorite delicacies has never been easier.
