Frequently Asked Questions

1. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa

2. How long does delivery take?

1. What is JFC Delicacy?

JFC Delicacy is an online food marketplace that offers a wide range of high-quality, fresh, and delicious food products. We provide everything from organic produce and ready-to-eat meals to gourmet ingredients and baked goods.

2. How do I place an order?

Placing an order is simple! Browse our product catalog, add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, enter your details, and make a payment. Once confirmed, you will receive an email with your order details.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods, including M-Pesa, credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and PayPal. All transactions are processed securely to ensure your payment details are protected.

4. Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer reliable delivery services across multiple locations. Delivery times may vary based on your location, and you can track your order status in real-time.

5. Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. Contact our customer support team immediately for assistance.

6. What is your return and refund policy?

We offer returns on eligible products within 7 days of delivery. Refunds are processed once the returned item has been inspected. Some perishable food items may not be eligible for returns.

7. Do you have a physical store?

No, we are an online-only store. However, we ensure timely deliveries and work with local suppliers to provide the freshest food items.

8. How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email. You can use this link to monitor the status of your delivery in real-time.

9. Is my personal information secure?

Yes! We take data privacy seriously. Your personal details are protected with industry-standard encryption, and we do not share your information with third parties without consent.

10. Can I create an account to save my order history?

Absolutely! Creating an account allows you to save your details, track orders, and receive special discounts and promotions.

11. Do you offer discounts or promotions?

Yes! We frequently run special promotions and discounts. Subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media to stay updated.

12. What should I do if I receive a wrong or damaged order?

If you receive the wrong order or damaged items, please contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as soon as possible.

13. Can I order in bulk for events or catering?

Yes! We provide bulk ordering options for special events, parties, and catering services. Contact us directly to discuss your requirements and pricing.

14. Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! We offer digital gift cards that can be purchased and sent to your loved ones. These gift cards can be redeemed for any item on our website.

15. Do you have a loyalty program?

We are working on a loyalty program to reward our customers for their continued support. Stay tuned for exciting rewards and benefits!
